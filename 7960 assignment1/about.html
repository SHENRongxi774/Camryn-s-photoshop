Personal Introduction

I am a student with a passion for photography and am currently exploring the infinite possibilities of light, composition, and storytelling. Although I'm just starting out, my passion for capturing moments and the pursuit of beauty have kept me learning and growing.My photography journey began at the end of 2024 when I picked up my camera and tried to capture the beautiful moments in my life. That's when I learned how to express emotions and tell stories through the lens. I love to shoot portraits and street shots, and I want to convey warmth and strength through my work.

Currently, I am studying Communication Studies at Hong Kong Baptist University and am constantly improving my photography skills through online courses and practice. I believe that photography is not only the accumulation of technology, but also the observation and perception of life.

If you like my work, or want to exchange photography ideas together, please feel free to contact me! Email: 24456098@life.hkbu.edu.hk
